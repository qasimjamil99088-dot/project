Science Program

The Science Program at Impact Academy offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers various scientific disciplines, including physics, chemistry, and biology. Students will engage in hands-on experiments and research projects to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Mathematics Program

The Mathematics Program at Impact Academy focuses on building a strong foundation in mathematical concepts and techniques. Students will explore topics such as algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics, preparing them for advanced studies and careers in STEM fields.

Arts Program

The Arts Program at Impact Academy encourages creativity and self-expression through various artistic mediums, including visual arts, music, and performing arts. Students will have the opportunity to develop their artistic talents and showcase their work in exhibitions and performances.

Technology Program

The Technology Program at Impact Academy provides students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of technology. Students will learn about programming, web development, robotics, and other emerging technologies, preparing them for careers in the tech industry.
